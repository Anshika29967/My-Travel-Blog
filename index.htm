My Adventure in Iceland

I recently fulfill my dream of visiting Iceland! This fascinating country is a land of extremes - from glaciers and volcanoes to hot springs and lava fields. Some highlights from my trip

  • Hiked up to see the stunning Skogafoss waterfall which drops 60m over a cliff
  • Went snowmobiling on the Myrdalsjokull glacier under the moonlight
  • Relaxed in the warm,mineral-rich waters of the Blue Lagoon geothermal spa
  • Watched the mesmerizing Northern Light dance across the night sky

I found the Icelandic people to be friendly and welcoming. The country's natural scenery is out of the world. Exploring the volcanic landscape makes you feel like you are on another planet! I can wait to return to Iceland someday.

The Foodie's Guide to Italy

They say the best part of traveling in Italy is the food - and I wholeheartedly agree! Here are same highlights from my culinary adventures in Italy.

  • Tried traditional Neopolitan pizza with fresh buffalo mozzarella cheese and sweet San Marzano tomatoes
  • Sampled the finest authentic pasta dishes like cacio e pepe, carbonara and bolognese
  • Indulged in scoops of velvety gelato in vibrant flavours like pistachio, stracciatella and tiramissu
  • Sipped refreshing Aperol Spritz cocktails and Italian wines like chianti in streetside cafes

From the Mediterranean-inspired cusine of south to the hearty meat and cheese dishes of the north, every region in Italy has something unique and honor the freshest local ingredients. I can't wait to return and discover more regional specialities!

Blog - 3

Wine Tasting in France

I had an amazing time touring the vineyards and wineries of France! As a wine lover, it was a dream to visit some of the world's most renowned wine regions.

In Bordeaux, I sampled incredible Cabernets and Merlots while soaking in the charming chateaux and medieval villages along the river. In Burgundy, I biked around quaint towns and tasted dazzling Pinot Noirs and Chardonnayas. My favorite was Champagne, where touring the underground cellars and sipping bubbly was the perfect end to my wine adventures.

With its dedication to terroir and winemaking tradition, France offers an unparalleld wine tourism experience. I can't wait to return and discover more hidden gems!
